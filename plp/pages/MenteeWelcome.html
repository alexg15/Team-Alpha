Professional Leadership Program

Thank you for your interest in becoming a student member of the UNT Professional Leadership Program for the Fall 2023 semester.

The Professional Leadership Program has transitioned into a one-credit-hour class course (BUSI 4980), offered semesterly. This application serves as a screening tool to identify student candidates that meet the qualifications to receive a membership interview. Once you are accepted into the program, you will receive your registration code to enroll in the course through the university system. This course is one-credit-hour and like all university courses, requires tuition payment. This course will appear in your Canvas dashboard.

Application Requirements

In order to apply for the professional leadership program, you must meet the following requirements:
  • Cumulative GPA of 3.25 or higher
  • Sophomore, junior, senior, or graduate student as of Fall 2023
  • Coachable mentality
  • Enrolled at UNT for the duration of the program

Member Commitments

You should only apply to the program if you are confident in your ability to commit to the following (if accepted into membership):

  • Committed to the full semester (Fall 2023)
  • Enrolled in the BUSI 4980 course through the university system
  • Mandatory weekly attendance to PLP class on Wednesday from 5:00 - 6:20 p.m. Beginning in Fall 2023
  • Completion of required community engagement hours each semester
  • Attendance to mandatory networking events
  • Consistent, monthly meetings with assigned mentor
  • Participation in supplemental opportunities and events
  • Professional representation of UNT and the Professional Leadership Program at all times in accordance with the entire UNT Code of Student Conduct. For example, see policy number 07.012: "Students and student groups are expected to conduct themselves in a manner that demonstrates respect for the rights and property of others and upholds the integrity and values of the University community."

Application process

If you meet the minimum application requirements and can fulfill the member commitments, you are welcome to apply for the Professional Leadership Program.

Please note that there are five application sections to complete. It should take you 15-30 minutes, depending on your background.

  • Apply Online - Applications close on Sunday, March 19, 2023
  • Interviews - Students who are selected to progress in the selection process will participate in an interview. Details TBA.
  • Mentor Match - Student members will be paired with a mentor in late summer 2023 and are encouraged to begin meeting.
  • First Member Meeting - The first PLP meeting of the semester will take place on Wednesday during the first week of classes in August 2023. The meeting location is TBA and will take place from 5:00 - 6:20 p.m.
